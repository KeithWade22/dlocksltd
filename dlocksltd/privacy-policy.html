Introduction

D-Locks Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to treating your personal data responsibly. This policy sets out the manner in which we collect, process, and secure your details when you are visiting our site. We observe the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and applicable privacy legislation.

What Information We Collect

We could collect the following categories of personal data:

  • First and last names, and contact information, i.e., e-mail and phone number.
  • Details that we receive from our contact forms or other engagement.
  • Technological data, such as IP address, browser type, and usage habits.
  • Any other information you opt to provide to us.

How We Use Your Information

We gather and use personal information for the below purposes:

  • To send messages about our services to you.
  • To address questions or requests.
  • For improving our website to give you a seamless experience.
  • To fulfil legal requirements where needed.

How We Store and Keep Your Information Safe

We have proper measures in place to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or loss. Your data is kept safely and retained only for so long as needed to achieve the purposes for which it was collected.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or trade personal data. We may, however, pass information to trusted third parties where necessary for operational purposes or in accordance with law. Data will be passed on under confidentiality agreements if data is disclosed.

Your Rights

You have rights under UK GDPR. These rights include:

  • Access the personal data we hold on you.
  • Seek corrections for inaccurate information.
  • Request your data to be deleted in specific situations.
  • Withdraw the consent to the processing of personal data where required.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website relies on cookies for improving user experience and analysing traffic to our site. You can control the cookie preferences via your browser settings. For further information, see our Cookies Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We might from time to time update this Privacy Policy to correspond with changes to our practices or legal requirements. Any such change will be placed on this webpage.

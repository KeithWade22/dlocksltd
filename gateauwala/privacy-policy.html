Privacy Policy

At Gateauwala, we are concerned about your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you entrust to us while visiting our website. Through interacting with our site, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described below. Our goal is to make your online experience with us as transparent and secure as possible.

Information We Gather

When you order or register for an update, we may gather some of the following information that concerns you:

  • Your name
  • Contact information such as email address and phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Preference for payment

In addition to the above information, our website collects non-personal information, including the type of browser used, the type of device, and how often you visit us. We do this to enhance the user experience.

How We Utilize Your Information

The information we gather will be used to:

  • Process your orders and make arrangements for delivery
  • Keep you updated on changes or alterations to your orders
  • Improve and customize your surfing experience
  • Learn how our visitors use our website to make it better

Don't worry. Your information is not sold or rented to unrelated third parties for unrelated purposes either.

Information Sharing

We may disclose or share your information with trusted third parties, only when necessary. This would include but shall not be limited to:

  • Delivery partners for a timely delivery of cakes to your doorstep.
  • Service providers who help manage our website or email communications

We require these parties to handle your information responsibly and use it solely for the intended purpose.

Data Security

We keep working hard to secure your data, using such secure practices as encryption and limited access to your personal information to prevent unauthorized access. However, while we are striving to protect your information, no online system can guarantee that your information is entirely safe and secure. We encourage you to take precautions, such as safeguarding your account details.

Your Rights

You control how much personal information you give to us. If you want to change or delete your data, or if you have concerns about how we handle your data, you can write to us at support@gateauwala.com and we will make every effort to respond to you promptly and respectfully.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to track visitors to our site. For more information, please see our Cookies Policy.

Third-Party Links

Our website includes links to third-party websites. We provide links for your convenience to information and services on an as-available basis. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party websites. You should review their policies before providing them with personal information.

Revisions to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect new practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically for updates. Continued use of this website after changes becomes tantamount to acceptance of the revised policy.

Thanks for trusting Gateauwala. We try to make your online interactions with us as safe and transparent as possible. If you have any questions or concerns, please email us at support@gateauwala.com.

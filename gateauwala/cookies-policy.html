Cookies Policy

We believe in transparency related to the data we collect and how we use the same at Gateauwala. This Cookies Policy aims to inform you about cookies' use by us on our website with full disclosure of our practices whenever you visit and interact with our online store. By accessing our website, you consent to our use of cookies as outlined below. 

What Are Cookies? 

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They enable websites to identify your device, including preferences, and allow a more enjoyable experience while browsing the website. In a nutshell, cookies will help our site function better, provide better interaction for you, and improve the way our website performs.

How We Utilize Cookies 

Cookies keep track of how you'll browse our website, which parts interest you majorly, and so on. They allow us to remember your preferences and settings so that you won't have to re-enter them with each visit. We also use them to track your browsing habits so that we can learn a lot about improving the performance of our website. 

The following are some of the types of cookies we may use: 

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies enable the operation of the website. They let you navigate the site and, for example, make an order.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are frequently visited. This helps us to evaluate the anatomy of our site and make improvements accordingly. 
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember your choices, such as your language or location, to provide a more personalized experience during your visit. 
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to display ads or content that is more relevant to you. They track your browsing habits across various websites. 

Managing Cookies 

You can always control your cookies. You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Remember that by blocking certain cookies you may not be able to take full advantage of all the features on our site. To learn more about how to change your cookie settings visit the help section of your browser. 

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services, such as analytics providers and advertisers, to place cookies on your device. These third-party cookies may track your online activity across different sites to provide personalized content and ads. We do not control the use of these cookies, so we encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third parties that may be collecting information about your visit. 

Your Privacy Rights 

We respect your privacy and take responsible measures to protect your data. In line with ideal practice, you may decline cookies at any time. Some cookies are necessary for basic site functionality and cannot be declined. If you have any concerns or questions about how we use cookies, please do not hesitate to contact us at support@gateauwala.com. 

Revisions to This Policy 

We reserve the right to change this Cookies Policy from time to time without notice. You should check back periodically to review the latest version. Your continued use of our website after we post any modifications shall be deemed to be your acceptance of the revised policy. 

Thank you for your trust in Gateauwala. We are committed to providing you with an uninterrupted online shopping experience while honoring your desire to control your private information.
